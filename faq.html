Frequently Asked Questions

  • What is Erasmus program and how does it work?

    The Erasmus program is a European Union initiative that provides opportunities for students to study or intern in other European countries. It aims to enhance the quality and reinforce the European dimension of higher education, and to promote intercultural understanding among students.

  • Who is eligible to apply for the Erasmus program?

    To be eligible for the Erasmus program, students must be enrolled in a higher education institution that is a part of the program, and they must have completed at least one year of study. They must also meet the academic and language requirements set by their home institution and the host institution.

  • What are the required documents for the application?
    1. CV
    2. Motivation Letter
    3. Copy of Passport
    4. Proof of Residence
    5. Diploma
    6. English Proficiency Certificate

  • When do applications open and close?

    Erasmus application dates may vary each year and these dates are determined by the Erasmus coordinator of your university. You can obtain information about application dates by checking regularly with your university's Erasmus coordinator or website regarding the application period.

  • What is the selection process for Erasmus program?

    The selection process for Erasmus program may vary depending on the university and country. Generally, selection is based on academic achievement, language proficiency, motivation letter, and interview. Final decisions are made by the university's Erasmus office and the host institution.

  • What do I need to do if I am accepted?

    During your preparation for Erasmus program, if accepted, you need to obtain information from your university's Erasmus coordinator and make necessary arrangements, such as visa procedures, travel arrangements, accommodation arrangements, and health insurance.

  • What do I need to do during my stay abroad?

    During your time abroad, you need to take necessary measures to protect your own health and safety. Additionally, you must fulfill the program requirements determined by your university, follow your academic obligations, and prepare the required reports at the end of the program.

  • What are the advantages of the Erasmus program?

    The Erasmus program offers many advantages to students, such as experiencing cultural differences, improving language skills, making new friends, and exploring new countries. It can also help you gain an international perspective, develop skills that employers prefer, and take a step ahead in your career.

  • Is financial support provided for the Erasmus student exchange program?

    Financial support can be provided to students under the Erasmus program, including grants that cover expenses such as travel, accommodation, and meals, as well as monthly payments. The type and amount of support may vary depending on the country and duration of the program.

  • What will happen to my health insurance during my stay abroad?

    During your study period abroad, you need to check if your health insurance is valid. You can also benefit from the health services provided by your university or obtain private health insurance.
